Quiz 1a: The difference between function setup() and function draw().

1. Update the sketch below to give your canvas a blue background in the setup() function.

Next, draw a rectangle that follows your mouse across the canvas. Need a hint? Check out mouseX and mouseY.

2. Update the sketch below to give your canvas a blue background in the draw() function.

Next, draw a rectangle that follows your mouse across the canvas.

Notice any differences?!


The setup() function repeats the code within it's curly brackets only once. Since the background is drawn on the canvas only once in this case, this results in the rectangle leaving behind a trail at every location that your mouse travels to.

The draw() function repeats the lines of code within its curly brackets constantly, which is why the rectangle following your mouse does not leave a trail of rectangles behind.
